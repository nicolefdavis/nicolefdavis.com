Selected Campaigns

  • Campaign Show Your Money Who's Boss
    Campaign Overview

    Developed a turnkey campaign Nationwide sales agents could easily use to attract and help CD-owning customers asses if a CD is their best investing vehicle.

    Goals

    Awareness: Make agents aware of marketing resources available to help them build their financial services business.

    Agent Activation: Get agents to activate the campaign. This will in turn make their clients aware that they offer other financial solutions and will also lead to clients considering the best investment vehicles to meet their needs.

    Content Execution

    1. Launch email 
    2. Marketing Sales Guide
    3. Marketing Storefront material  (online) - Table tents, lanyard, wall clings, print ads, postcards, customer emails, letters and envelopes

    Measurement & Evaluation

    Awareness: We saw high email open and click through rates which demonstrated that many agents were becoming aware of the campaign.
    - Email open rate — 58.9% (exceeded company average of approximately 20%)
    - Email click through rate — 5% (exceeded company average of approximately 2%)

    Agent Activation: Orders of the campaign materials show 229 agents activated the campaign (an activation rate of 25%).

  • Campaign AALU Conference Experience
    Campaign Overview

    Create an AALU conference experience that would inform advisors that Nationwide can help them provide customized life insurance solutions that meet the needs of both individual clients and business clients who want to attract and retain employees.

    Goals

    Awareness (primary): Make financial advisors aware that Nationwide can help them deliver on the life insurance needs of both individual and business clients.

    Consideration (secondary): Increase consideration of selling Nationwide life insurance individual and business products

    Content Execution

    1. Booth design and setup 
    2. Promotional material in survival kit bag
    3. Pre- and Post-conference emails

    Measurement & Evaluation

    Awareness:
    - Built awareness with more than 250 attendees
    - 85% of survival kits distributed
    - Pre-conference Email open rate — 22.9%; Email click through rate — 4%
    - Post-conference Email open rate — 35%; Email click through rate — 6%

    Consideration:  
    Built consideration of life insurance solutions; 124 email signups - request for marketing material.
